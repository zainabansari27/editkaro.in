Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a video editing project?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, length, and required effects. Short-form videos typically take 2-3 days, while more complex projects like documentaries or long-form content may take 1-2 weeks. We'll provide a specific timeline during our initial consultation.

What file formats do you accept for raw footage?

We accept a wide range of video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, MPEG, and more. If you have footage in a specific format, please let us know, and we'll accommodate your needs.

Do you provide revisions after the initial edit?

Yes, our packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result. Additional revision rounds can be arranged if needed.

Can you help with content strategy for my social media?

Absolutely! Our digital marketing services include content strategy development tailored to your brand, audience, and business goals. We can help plan, create, and optimize content for maximum engagement.

Do you offer rush services for urgent projects?

Yes, we offer expedited services for clients with tight deadlines. Rush fees may apply depending on the project scope and timeline. Contact us with your requirements, and we'll do our best to accommodate your schedule.
